Steaks

There are many different cuts of steaks. My personal favorite is a ribeye. Other popular cuts are filet mignon and New York strip. T-Bone is actually a filet and a New York strip separated by a bone in the shape of a T.

There are even steaks for vegans.

  • Cauliflower
  • Tofu
  • Eggplant
